We Know

Substance abuse is a problem that negativeley impacts idividuals, families, and communities.

Our Mission

Is to develop individualized treatment that restores those struggling with addiction to a meaningful life where they will flourish as contributing members of a family, church, workplace, and community.

Our Vision

Is that recovery is possible! Restored will treat the whole person. We will address the root of the problem, rather than just the symptoms.

The Problem

1,526 fatal drug overdoses in Indiana in 2016.

Annual cost to Indiana is more than $12 Billion in the form of health care, tax burdens, loss of productivity, crime, overdose deaths & more.

10%

The estimated number of Indiana residents with a substance issue.

1 in 3 babies born in Indiana test positive for illicit drugs.

Our Approach

Restored is an Amen Clinics affiliate. We will use the Amen Clinics Method, 4 Circles Approach, to find a natural solution to boost the brain and put you on the path to recovery. We have designed our intake process to look at each of the factors. This allows us to truly understand you.

BIOLOGICAL

We want to know about your genetics, overall health, diet, exercise, and any history of brain injury or exposure to toxins.

PSYCHOLOGICAL

We’ll explore your thinking patterns and ask if you had any significant developmental events or emotional traumas in the past.

SOCIAL

We’ll ask what your relationships with friends and family are like, if you have a lot of stressors and if you spend time with others or feel isolated.

SPIRITUAL

We want you to know that God cares for you!
